Raila Amolo Odinga

A Life of Leadership, Legacy & Democracy

Biography & Achievements

  • Born on 7 January 1945 in Maseno, Kenya.
  • Son of Jaramogi Oginga Odinga — an important figure in Kenya’s fight for independence.
  • Pioneered the push for multiparty democracy in the 1990s.
  • Served as Prime Minister of Kenya from 2008-2013.
  • Contested several Presidential elections, shaping Kenya’s modern political landscape.
  • Passed away on 15 October 2025; remembered nationwide for his voice, service, and courage.
